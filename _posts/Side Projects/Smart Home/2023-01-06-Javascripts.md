<span style="color:#3ababa">Promises</span> are objects that represent the not-yet-available result of an asynchronous operation. The keywords <span style="color:#3ababa">async</span> and <span style="color:#3ababa">awaitsync</span> simplify asynchronous programming by allowing you to structure your Promise-based code as if it was synchronous.
